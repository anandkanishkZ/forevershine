Post-Delivery Technical Inspection - Federal Parliament Secretariat

October 6, 2025•By Forever Shine Engineering Team
Back to Blog

Site visit conducted for preparation of Post Delivery Technical Inspection Report following the supply of Furniture & Furnishings Goods to the Federal Parliament Secretariat (संघीय संसद सचिवालय). Project Details: • Client: Federal Parliament Secretariat • Department: Internal Service and Plan Management Administration (आन्तरिक सेवा तथा योजना शाखा) • Location: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu, Nepal • Scope: Post-delivery technical inspection of furniture and furnishings Our inspection process included: • Quality verification of delivered furniture items • Installation standards and finish quality assessment • Compliance with government procurement specifications • Functional testing of all furniture components • Documentation of any defects or required adjustments • Warranty and maintenance guidelines preparation This prestigious government project demonstrates our capability in handling high-profile institutional projects requiring meticulous attention to detail and compliance with government standards. Working with Nepal's Federal Parliament showcases our trusted position in delivering professional services to the nation's most important institutions, ensuring the highest levels of quality and reliability in our technical assessments.
